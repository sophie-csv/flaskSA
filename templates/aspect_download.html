What is aspect-based sentiment analysis?

Aspect-based sentiment analysis looks at specific parts (aspects) of a topic to understand what people like or dislike about each one. For restaurants, this can reveal opinions on food quality, service, ambiance, and price, helping identify what customers value most and where improvements can be made.

What does this mean?

The "Density of the Compound Sentiment Scores by Aspect" graph displays the distribution of sentiment/polarity scores (-1 for negative and 1 for positive) for specific aspects of a topic and how often it appears within the dataset. The y-axis shows the density, indicating how often sentiment scores appear, while the x-axis represents the compound sentiment score, ranging from positive to negative. This graph helps visualize which aspects are more positively or negatively discussed and how frequently those sentiments occur.
